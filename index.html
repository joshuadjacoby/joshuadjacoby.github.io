About

My name is Josh Jacoby, and I am about to graduate from the University of California, Irvine with a B.S. in Software Engineering and a passion for video game development.

I have wanted to be a video game developer ever since I took a Computer Science course in High School and realized that I could combine my ability to program with my love for video games. Because I have such a drive to do this, I have taken every opportunity I could find at UC Irvine to prepare myself for working in the industry, from working on side projects alone as well as with others in the Video Game Development Club, to taking optional courses in topics ranging from game writing and sound design to networked multiplayer game programming.

I love the freedom programming gives me to bring new ideas to life on the computer screen. My passion is video game development and I am looking to obtain a game programming position at a company pushing the boundaries of modern gaming forward. If I am not making the next big game, I am often playing one. Right now my favorites include PUBG, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and Mario + Rabbids Kingdom Battle.
