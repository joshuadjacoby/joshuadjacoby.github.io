About

My name is Josh Jacoby, and I am a recent graduate from the University of California, Irvine with a B.S. in Software Engineering.

I love that programming gives me the freedom to bring new ideas to life. My passion is using software to improve the lives of others and solve real world problems. I am looking to obtain a software engineer position with an emphasis on front-end development at a company that constantly aims to push the boundaries of what technology can do for us.

If I am not working on the next big thing, I am often playing video games, exploring various destinations, or trying new foods.

Rick O'Shea

Previous Next

Rick O'Shea is a game developed using Unity2D. For this project, the goal was to create a top down shooter that was different than any we had seen before. Taking inspiration from games such as Hotline Miami and Enter the Gungeon, Rick O'Shea adds onto this classic formula by using a ricochet mechanic for the bullets, which can kill both enemies as well as the player. This creates a whole new level of difficulty forcing the player to be extra careful with each shot they take.

  • Gameplay Programming
  • Game Design
